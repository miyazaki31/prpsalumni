Relief activities for flood victims

The PRPS Alumni Association raised 76,570 BDT to aid flood victims in Bangladesh. The funds were donated to the Chief Adviser's Relief and Welfare Fund to be distributed to those affected. The association hopes to alleviate the suffering of flood victims and build a more compassionate society.

PRPS Cricket Carnival 1

PRPS Cricket Carnival 1 was a huge success due to the participation of ex-batch students from Batch 2019 to Batch 2024. Batch 21 (PRPS KINGS) won the championship, and Batch 22 (PRPS Hurricanes) was the runner-up. The principal of Pran-RFL Public School, Mr. Shah Mohammad Jewell Reza Sir, was the chief guest of the event.
