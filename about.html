Who We Are

The PRPS Alumni Association is a vibrant community that provides a range of services and opportunities for our members to stay connected to the School and each other. We partner with the school administration to cultivate meaningful connections with our alumni, organize engaging events, and create opportunities for graduates to contribute to the school's continued success. To ensure we have your most up-to-date information, we encourage you to update your profile on the PRPS Alumni Portal. As a member of the PRPS Alumni Association, you enjoy a variety of benefits and programs, including:


✧ Exclusive Access: Enjoy exclusive on- and off-campus benefits and services.
✧ Career Advancement: Benefit from career programs tailored to your needs.
✧ Reunion Experiences: Participate in memorable reunions and stimulating chapter events.
✧ Networking Opportunities: Connect with fellow alumni and industry professionals.
✧ Philanthropic Impact: Contribute to the success of current students through fundraising efforts.
